Alive

Alive is a Korean Zombie released in 2020. It is about a young man named Oh Joon-woo who gets trapped alone in his apartment in Seoul.
A sudden outbreak turns people into fast, dangerous zombies. The movie shows his fight to survive, not just from the zombies but also from
being completely lonely and running out of food and water. He later finds another survivor, Kim Yoo-bin, in a nearby building. They work together
to try to stay alive. Many people liked the movie for its tense and emotional story. Some people felt partsof the story were predictable, but overall it
was a popular and well-received film.
